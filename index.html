About Us

Since our modest beginnings in 2005 with a little space in Toronto’s stylish Yorkville locale, ‘Food Network’ ‘s development has been enlivened with the energy to cook and serve solid, Bangladeshi-roused takeout food. In contrast to other Bangladesh eateries, ‘Food Network’ was made with the explicit expectation to appear as something else.
